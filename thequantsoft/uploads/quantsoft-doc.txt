PRISM

AI-Powered Intelligence Infrastructure for Financial Research

PRISM is an intelligent research and workflow system designed to reduce the operational burden across institutional financial analysis.

Built by Quantsoft, PRISM helps analysts, investment teams, and financial professionals process information faster, track businesses more effectively, and transform fragmented financial data into structured intelligence.

The Problem

Modern financial research remains heavily operational.

Analysts spend a significant amount of time:

rManually extracting KPIs across dozens of filings

Tracking regulatory updates across multiple companies and sectors

Reorganizing notes and summaries for every new report cycle

Re-reading the same information across disconnected sources

The result? Your best people spend less time thinking and more time processing.

PRISM is built to change that

What is PRISM?

PRISM is an AI-powered financial intelligence system designed to augment research workflows through automation, structured intelligence, and intelligent retrieval.

Rather than functioning as a generic chatbot, PRISM is being developed as a workflow-oriented intelligence layer for financial analysis.

Core Capabilities

Financial Filing Analysis

Analyze annual reports, quarterly filings, presentations, and disclosures with AI-assisted understanding and contextual retrieval.

KPI &amp; Financial Extraction

Extract important metrics, financial indicators, and operational insights from large financial documents.

Business &amp; Sector Intelligence

Track companies, industries, competitors, and business developments across multiple information sources.

Research Workflow Assistance

Assist analysts in organizing information, generating summaries, preparing notes, and accelerating repetitive research workflows.

Regulatory &amp; Market Monitoring

Monitor disclosures, filings, updates, and evolving developments relevant to financial decision systems.

Knowledge Layer for Financial Teams

Build a centralized intelligence layer capable of connecting fragmented financial information into structured workflows.

Why PRISM Matters

Every hour your analyst spends extracting data is an hour not spent interpreting it. Every filing they miss is information a competitor may have acted on. Every disconnected system in your stack is friction that slows your edge.

PRISM is built to reduce that friction — so your team's time goes where it creates the most value.
